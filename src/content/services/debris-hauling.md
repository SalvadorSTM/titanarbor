## Cleanup is part of the job

Every tree job we do includes cleanup — chipping branches, raking debris, and leaving the work area neat. Hauling logs and chips off-site is a separate option, but one most customers want.

## When standalone hauling makes sense

- Storm dropped a mess in your yard and you just need it gone
- Previous tree work left logs and brush you never finished dealing with
- A neighbor's tree came down on your side of the property line
- Yard cleanup after a renovation, move-out, or land clearing by someone else

We can come out, assess the volume, and haul it the same day or the next.

## What we take

Wood, branches, brush, leaves, and other organic yard debris. We sort what's chippable from what gets hauled as log lengths. Wood goes to firewood, mulch, or biomass — nothing usable ends up in a landfill.

What we don't take: construction debris, household trash, painted or treated lumber, fence posts that aren't tree-related. For those you need a regular junk hauler.
