## When the wind brings one down

A tree on the house, the car, the fence, the driveway, or hung up dangerously in another tree — these don't wait for business hours. Diego is on call 24/7 for storm work. Call **478-268-8020** any hour.

## What we prioritize, in this order

1. **Life safety** — get people away from anything that could fall further
2. **Structure protection** — stabilize what's already on your home before more damage occurs
3. **Access restoration** — clear blocked driveways, doorways, and utility runs
4. **Full removal & cleanup** — once it's safe, the rest of the job

## What to do before we arrive

- Get everyone out of the affected area, including out of the rooms below or near anything hanging
- Do not touch downed power lines or trees in contact with them — call your utility first, then us
- Don't try to cut anything yourself if it's under load (bent, pinned, hung up). A loaded tree releases violently when cut wrong, and storm wood is the most common cause of serious chainsaw injuries
- Take photos for your insurance claim if it's safe to do so

## Insurance claims

Most storm-related tree damage that hits your home, garage, fence, or vehicle is covered by homeowners' insurance. We can document the damage, provide an itemized estimate suitable for your adjuster, and coordinate timing with the claim. Diego will walk you through it.
